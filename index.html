Collin is a software engineer, theatre music director, and cat dad living with his fiancée in Denver, CO.

He currently works at KSAT (Kongsberg Satellite Services) as a software integration and test engineer by day and collaborates with several local community theatre companies as a music director, pianist, and conductor after working hours.

Collin enjoys working on personal software projects, various forms of cardio exercise (e.g. running and biking), reading (mostly thriller/horror), and being in the wilderness (esp. getting on top of mountains).
